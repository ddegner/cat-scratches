## Find your name, find your brand

A good business name helps you establish credibility, differentiate yourself from the competition, and connect with your target audience. Some advance thinking can help you identify the right name for your business.

Know your brand

Identify the fundamental elements of your brand: values, personality, and goals. Understanding the foundation of your business will help guide your name choice.

Pinpoint your audience

Understanding who you’re speaking to with your brand is crucial. How does your audience communicate? How do you want them to feel?

Gather feedback

Getting some external input from people who represent your target audience can help you decide what to focus on. They can also offer an unbiased opinion on your top business name ideas.

Using our small business name generator can help you discover just the right handle for your next venture, but here are some criteria to keep in mind when making your final selection:

Simplicity

Short, straightforward names are the easiest to remember.

Relevance

Names that reflect your products or services will help people understand your offerings.

Uniqueness

Distinctive names are less likely to be confused with other brands.

Memorability

Easy-to-remember names will stick in people’s minds.
