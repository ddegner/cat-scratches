Wild in a 0-2 hole in 2nd-round series, special teams slump deepens ahead of Game 3 against Colorado

The Minnesota Wild left Denver in an 0-2 hole and amid an 0-for-5 power-play slump in the series

May 6, 2026 at 4:14 p.m. EDT6 minutes ago
By Pat Graham | AP

DENVER — The Minnesota Wild left Denver in an 0-2 hole and amid an 0-for-5 power-play slump in the series.

It’s not difficult for the Wild to zero in on what needs to turn around in the off days before the second-round series with Colorado resumes Saturday for Game 3 in St. Paul, Minnesota.
