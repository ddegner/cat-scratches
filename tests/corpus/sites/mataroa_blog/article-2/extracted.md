After a long and arduous journey that lasted more than two years, 5399 Mataroa blogs have been manually and carefully screened. 54% of them were rubbish and have been deleted. 2868 terrible blogs have been deleted. No empty blogs have been deleted, nor any blogs which have one post titled "Test". These are by honest humans and we want to keep them.
