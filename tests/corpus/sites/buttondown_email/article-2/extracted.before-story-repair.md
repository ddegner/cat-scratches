## What we _don't_ do:

With our exclusive focus on email, we currently don't handle omnichannel communications. Fortunately, our integrations and API let you connect to other focused tools.

Because we're focused on sending out the best possible email, we tend not to focus on every specific need of any one platform. We've found this trade-off results in our having a more stable, durable business, which allows us to serve our existing customers as well as possible. If we're missing a specific feature that you find you might need, don't hesitate to reach out.
