Brunch Spinach Salad

4.9
(29)

3 Photos

My coworker Lori makes this brunch salad and we all absolutely love it! It's quick and easy with absolutely no cooking required. Great during the warm summer months.

  • 2 (10 ounce) bags baby spinach

  • 2 bananas, sliced

  • 12 fresh strawberries, sliced

  • 1 (10 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained

  • ½ cup dried cranberries

  • ½ cup pine nuts

  • 1 cup poppy seed salad dressing

Directions

  1. Gather the ingredients.

  2. Place spinach in a large bowl. Arrange bananas, strawberries, mandarin oranges, cranberries, and pine nuts on top.

  3. Toss with poppy seed dressing before serving.
